This guide provides a detailed breakdown of **VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price)** and its practical applications in trading. VWAP calculates the average price of an asset weighted by trading volume, serving as **dynamic support and resistance**. The author, @extipumpBTC, explains several types of VWAP and how traders can use them across different timeframes and market contexts.

## 🔧 **Types of VWAP & Their Uses**

### 1. **Standard VWAP (Intraday/Session VWAP)**
- **Resets each trading day/session.**
- **Interpretation:** Price above VWAP → buyers in control; below → sellers in control.
- **Use:** Ideal for intraday trading; acts as a dynamic reference for trend bias.
![[Pasted image 20260110224030.png]]
### 2. **Anchored VWAP (AVWAP)**
- **Custom start point** (e.g., swing high/low, news day, breakout).
- **Interpretation:** Shows price reaction to volume since a key event. First touch often produces a strong bounce or rejection.
- **Use:** Great for swing trading; enter on first touch with stop above/below AVWAP.
![[Pasted image 20260110224018.png]]
### 3. **Rolling VWAP (RVWAP)**
- **Continuous VWAP** that doesn’t reset daily (e.g., 30D, 90D, 365D).
- **Interpretation:** Acts as mean reversion level; price often reacts at RVWAP retests.
- **Use:** Medium to higher timeframe analysis; avoid periods under 7 days.
![[Pasted image 20260110224002.png]]
### 4. **Multi-Period VWAP**
- **Auto-anchored** to periods: weekly, monthly, quarterly, yearly.
- **Interpretation:** Resets at period start; previous period VWAP becomes **static S/R** (e.g., pmVWAP = previous month VWAP).
- **Use:** Identifies structural levels across timeframes.
![[Pasted image 20260110224109.png]]
### 5. **VWAP with Bands**
- **Adds standard deviation bands** (±1, ±2) around VWAP.
- **Interpretation:** Bands indicate volatility; upper band = VAH (Value Area High), lower band = VAL (Value Area Low).
- **Use:** Identifies overextended moves; bands act as dynamic S/R. Often used with multi-period VWAPs.

## 📌 Key Insights

### ✅ **VWAP as a Dynamic Tool**
- Not just an indicator—a **real-time measure of volume-weighted price fairness**.
- Acts as **dynamic support/resistance** that adjusts with volume.

### 🎯 **Trading Applications**
- **Intraday:** Use standard VWAP to gauge intraday bias.
- **Swing Trading:** Use AVWAP anchored to key events for high-probability entries.
- **Higher Timeframe Analysis:** Use RVWAP and multi-period VWAP for trend analysis and mean reversion.
- **Confluence:** Combine VWAP with other tools like order flow, options data (OI), and market structure.

### 🛡️ **Risk & Entry Tactics**
- **Enter on first touch** of AVWAP/RVWAP with stop just beyond it.
- **Use VWAP bands** to identify extremes and potential reversals.
- **Previous period VWAPs** become static levels—useful for future S/R.

### 📊 **Setup Example from the Guide**
- **BTC Trade:** Long entry at bounce off **365D RVWAP + yearly VWAP**, take profit at monthly VWAP.
- **ETH Trade:** Short entry on rejection at **90D RVWAP** with confluence of high OI and 30D RVWAP below 90D.

### 🔧 **Tool Recommendation**
- **Free Indicator:** _Koalafied VWAP_ on TradingView for multi-period VWAP with bands.
- **Chart Setup:** Use multiple panels with Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, and Yearly VWAPs for full context.
